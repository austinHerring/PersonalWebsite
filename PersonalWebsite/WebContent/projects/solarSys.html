Solar System Simulator

January, 2015

This project simply models our solar system in 2D. It keeps all of the plant statistics in ratio including obit speed, planet diameter and distance from the sun's core. The sun is excluded. When the planets orbit in real time, the movements are invisible to the human eye. For this reason I added a feature that speeds up the orbit so that the different planet rates are more apparent. In addition, it was hard to see the plants within the astroid belt because their sizes are so small compared to the outer planets. That is why I added the zoom feature. The source code can be downloaded here and ran locally.
